Create a Checklist

A checklist helps visualize a larger task into its components and substeps.

To create a checklist
  1. Click the Card
  2. Click Checklist
  3. Title the checklist and click Add
You can reuse a previously created checklist with the Drop Down Menu
  1. Once the checklist is created, add items to it
    1. In the checklist section of the card click Add an item
    2. Type the item name and click Add
    3. Optional: Assign the item to a team member by clicking @ and typing their name to tag them. This sends them a notification.
The card updates on the main board to reflect the new checklist tasks
