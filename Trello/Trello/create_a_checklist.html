Create a Checklist

A checklist helps break a larger task into its substeps.

  1. Click the Card.
  2. Click Checklist.
  3. Enter a title for the checklist and click Add.
    You can reuse a previously created checklist with the Drop Down Menu.
  4. After the checklist is created, add items to it.
    1. In the checklist section of the card, click Add an item.
    2. Type the item name and click Add.
    3. Optional: Assign the item to a team member by clicking @ and typing their name to tag them.
      The team member receives a message to notify them of the assigned item.
The card updates on the main board to reflect the new checklist tasks.
