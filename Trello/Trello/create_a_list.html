Create and Name Lists

Lists are the categories that you assign your cards and tasks.

Organize your cards by setting up lists to place them under. Trello is a very flexible application, so experiment with different categories to find the ones that best fit your needs.

To add a list

  1. Click the text field + Add another list
  2. Name your list and press Enter
    Give lists names such as "Things to Do" or "Done" to sort cards based on task progress.
Try out different names for your lists. Just click the list's name to rename it.
