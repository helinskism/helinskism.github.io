Convert a Checklist Item to a Card

Sometimes a checklist item may need its own card.

To convert an item
  1. Click the Card
  2. In the Checklist section of the card, navigate to an item and hover over it to reveal its settings icon ...
  3. Select ... > Convert to Card
The new card is added to the same list as the card it was created from
