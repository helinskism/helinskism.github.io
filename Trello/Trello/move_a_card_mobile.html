Move a Card Between Lists

Move cards to another list to stay organized.

There are two ways to move a card
  • Use the Move command in the menu
  • Tap and drag the card to another list
To use the Move command
  1. Tap the Card
  2. Select the settings option ... in the top right of the screen
  3. Select Move to open a series of menus where you will select where the card will move to
    1. Select the Board where your card will move to
    2. Select the List where your card will move to
    3. Select the order position where the card will appear in that list
    4. Tap Save

Dragging Cards

Alternatively, you can drag cards into new positions and lists

To drag a card
  1. Tap and hold on a Card
  2. Drag the Card into another list or reorder its position in the same list
Archive a card by dragging it to the top of the screen and releasing over the Drag here to archive field.
