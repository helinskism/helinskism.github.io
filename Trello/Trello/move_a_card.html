Move a Card Between Lists

Move cards to another list to stay organized.

There are two ways to move a card
  • Use the Move command in the menu
  • Click and drag the card to another list
To use the Move command
  1. Click the Card Settings button
  2. Select Move
  3. In the Move Card menu, select the new location of the card with the drop down menus
    OptionResult
    Board Move the card to an entirely new board
    List Choose which list the card will appear under
    Position Choose the position of where the card will move to in the desired list
  4. Click Move to finish

Dragging Cards

Alternatively, you can drag cards into new positions and lists

To drag a card
  1. Click and hold on a Card
  2. Drag the Card into another list or reorder its position in the same list
