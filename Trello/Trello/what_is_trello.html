Welcome to Trello

Trello is a web-based Kanban board that helps users organize their projects.

With Trello, you can create categories called lists that hold cards such as subjects and tasks. Many people use the application to visualize tasks and organize relevant information. You update your progress by marking tasks complete or by moving cards to another list.
