Create Your Board

After signing up for Trello, create your Trello board by completing the set-up wizard.

Create an account, then follow the on-screen instructions to populate your board.
Note: You must follow the set-up wizard to populate a board. To quickly get to a board, fill in any information and complete the wizard. After completing, edit your lists and cards to customize Trello.
  1. Sign up at http://Trello.com/signup.
  2. Enter a name for your board.
  3. Create your lists.
  4. Enter a name for your cards.
  5. Add sample details to the card.
  6. Click the Keep Building Your Board button to finish the wizard and proceed to your board.
