Comment on a Card

Leave comments on cards to update your progress.

Comments are made in the Activity field of a card.
  1. Click the Card.
  2. Type a comment in the Activity field.
    There are also several options in the bottom right of the comment field. Choices include:
    OptionDescription
    Attach a file to the comment.
    Tag a team member to send them a notification.
    Insert an emoji into the comment.
    Insert a shortcut to another card in the comment. Clicking the shortcut will take you to the corresponding card.
  3. When finished, click Save.
Click Show Details in the Activity field to view all activity on a card.
