Kanban

Kanban is a workflow management method that helps you visualize your work.

By using a Kanban system such as Trello, you can:
  • Visualize your workflow.
  • Prioritize tasks.
  • Provide feedback.
  • Work collaboratively.
Create a board to get started, then fill it with lists and cards to track the tasks of your project.
