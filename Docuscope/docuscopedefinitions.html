DocuScope Definitions

Refer to this page for definitions of different DocuScope elements

Term Definition
Dictionary A hand crafted database that is used to analyze the text. The default dictionary contains 40 million unique patterns of English classified into over 100 rhetorical functions.
Cluster Broad level category of language patterns.
Dimension Filters a cluster based on certain criteria.
Language Action Types (LATs) The lowest level category, LATs represent the smallest units of micro-rhetoric, words, and word sequences.
